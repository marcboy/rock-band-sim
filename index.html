Rock Band Sim

Manage a fictional rock band over a 10-year career. Write songs, record albums, book gigs, and watch every choice nudge two compounding numbers: albums sold and fan count. The loop compounds — or stalls, and you have to figure out why.

Open the prototype ▸ Read the design spec

What's playable now

Write & record

Build a song backlog, record demos, ship albums when you have 4+ tracks. Quality drives first-week sales.

The compounding loop

Sales feed fans. Fans feed bigger first-week sales on the next album. Cash from royalties funds the next session.

3 save slots

Run three independent careers in parallel. State persists in your browser between visits.

Live events

Sync deals, blog write-ups, gear failures — small choice cards that nudge the trajectory.
